Landon Green Program

The Landon Hotel - London was recently renovated, and we considered the impact on the earth the entire way. From green building materials, to solar power, to energy-friendly lighting and appliances throughout the hotel - we’re saving energy in every socket, outlet, and switch. We’ve also initiated a recycling and composting program that reduces the load to local landfills, while providing valuable raw material for use in new products, or in the case of compost, for use in local gardens and landscapes.

Dining Options

Here at the Landon Hotel, we want you to have every option available to you during your stay. If you're looking for a quick bite, a place to enjoy drinks with friends, or an elegant dinner for two, you can find it here. We offer dining experiences for every taste.

Room Service

Our room service menu is available 24 hours a day. You’ll find a variety of options available for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and even snacks. Our room service staff is happy to bring you a pot of coffee, a nightcap, or a full meal. If you’d like to have a private party, we can bring you everything you need to host a small event, or even deliver the party for larger events in our private banquet room.

The Rooftop Café & Smoothie Bar

Our Rooftop Café & Smoothie Bar is an experience all its own. Located on the top floor of the hotel, you'll find a collection of coffee drinks, smoothies, and light fare including sandwiches, salads, and snacks. We offer indoor seating in our cozy café, as well as seasonal outdoor seating for those who prefer to feel the wind through their hair.
